# Quickstart: Validate Navigation to Transactions

## Verification Guide
1. Launch the application `Student Finance & Cash Flow Management System`.
2. Wait for the `DashboardHomeView` (Tổng quan tài chính) to load and display data.
3. Locate the "Giao dịch gần đây" (Recent Transactions) section at the bottom left of the Dashboard.
4. Click the "Xem tất cả" (See all) button located at the top right of the "Giao dịch gần đây" section.
5. **Expected Outcome**: The main view should instantly switch to the "Quản lý giao dịch" (Transactions) view, and the "Giao dịch" item on the left sidebar should become highlighted as the active menu item.
